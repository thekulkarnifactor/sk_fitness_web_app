## Top 5 Quick Fixes (30 minutes)

### 1. Update Hero Headline
```
❌ "Fuel Your Journey. Customize Every Bite."
✅ "Smart Nutrition. Built for Your Goals."
```
**Impact**: Adds SEO keyword, clearer value prop

### 2. Shorten Subheadline
```
❌ "Build your own protein-rich meal. Choose your goal, cuisine,
    and ingredients — we'll handle the rest."
✅ "Create custom high-protein meals in minutes.
    Pick your cuisine. We calculate the macros."
```
**Impact**: 15 vs 17 words, more scannable, better keywords

### 3. Add Page Titles
```html
<title>AI Meal Planning for Athletes | House of Macros</title>
```
**Impact**: Immediate SEO boost

### 4. Break Long Paragraphs
- Max 3-4 sentences per paragraph
- Ideal: 2-3 sentences
- Use bullet points where possible

### 5. Simplify Feature Descriptions
```
❌ "Smart ingredient suggestions tailored to your fitness goals
    and macro targets"
✅ "Get AI-powered meal suggestions that match your goals and
    hit your macros perfectly"
```
**Impact**: 7th grade vs 12th grade reading level

## Sentence Length Rules

| Words | Rating | Use For |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 1-10 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Headlines, CTAs |
| 11-20 | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Body content |
| 21-25 | ⭐⭐ | Complex ideas (limit) |
| 26+ | ❌ | Never - break it up |

**Your Target**: 12-15 words average

## Active Voice Cheat Sheet

```
❌ PASSIVE                          ✅ ACTIVE
"Meals are designed"              → "Meals fuel your training"
"Ingredients are sourced"         → "We source ingredients"
"Should be tailored"              → "Tailor your nutrition"
"Food is performance-driven"      → "Performance-driven food"
```

**Test**: Can you add "by zombies"? If yes, it's passive!

## Emergency Fixes

If metrics drop suddenly:

1. **Check Google Search Console** - Any penalties?
2. **Test page speed** - Loading under 3 seconds?
3. **Review recent changes** - What changed?
4. **Check mobile version** - Working properly?
5. **Verify links** - All working?
